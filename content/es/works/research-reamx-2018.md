Founder member of REA_MX (Red Ecología Acústica_México). REA_MX is a pioneering network that fosters collaboration across disciplines and sectors within Acoustic Ecology in Mexico, bridging academia, artistic practice, and environmental advocacy.

Conceived as a platform for artists, researchers, and professionals, REA_MX supports a wide range of activities—from exhibitions to conferences—and advocates for critical and creative sonic practices in both national and international contexts.
