**BECKETT-MEXICO**

Founder and director of **BECKETT-MEXICO**, a curatorial, research, performance, and exhibition initiative launched in 2018 to foster collaboration among Mexican and Latin-American scholars and practitioners working on Samuel Beckett. The initiative has resulted in three international conferences and several dissemination projects. Key collaborators include Mexican institutions such as **Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México**, **Seminario de Cultura Mexicana**, and **Universidad Autónoma Metropolitana**, along with international partners like **The Samuel Beckett Society**, **Trinity College Dublin**, the **Irish Embassy of Mexico**, **Culture Ireland**, **Universidad de Almería**, and **Universidad de Buenos Aires**.

**Major International Conferences Organized by BECKETT-MEXICO:**

- **BECKETT-MEXICO [ENCOUNTER 02]** (Online, December 2020)  
- **BECKETT-MEXICO [ENCOUNTER 01]** (Mexico City, November 2019)  
- **4th International Conference of The Samuel Beckett Society: "Transdisciplinary Beckett"** (Mexico City, November 2018)  

After a productive period, the initiative paused in 2021.
